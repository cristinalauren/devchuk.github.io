About Me

Kevin Lee

I am a serial social entrepreneur and nonprofit activist. I believe in universal access to education and net neutrality.

I am the President of ACE Foundation International, a non-profit organization that facilitates access to education through North America and 11 other countries through our Mentoring & Tutoring Campaigns and our International Outreach Programs.

I serve on the Board of the Student Net Alliance as the Director of Administration and on Enterprize Vancouver as the Chief Operating Officer. I recently completed my rotations as Chief of Staff of SDC Blue Ribbon Foundation and as Deputy Secretary General of Canadian High Schools Model United Nations.

I am involved in the startup industry as a growth hacker and minor angel investor, and intern at a boutique private equity firm. In the past, I’ve interned at Google, Facebook Seattle, McCarthy Tétrault, and the Canadian Government.

In my spare time, I am a Coursera junkie and a pro bono consultant. I am currently learning the ropes of SEO/SEM marketing, and read Douglas Coupland and Gödel, Escher, Bach religiously. I also live for Ultimate Frisbee, and am the Director of Design for the Vancouver Nighthawks, an MLU team.

I write and slam poems, and will otherwise be seen tinkering with graphics on Photoshop. I am currently open to doing pro bono projects. You can find more pieces of me on LinkedIn.

My Occupations

Intern | International Alliance Associates

Founded in 1990, International Alliance Associates, (IAA) is a US-based specialized boutique private equity placement agent and cross-border strategic advisory firm with offices in New York, Riyadh and Bejing. Our core businesses focus on raising capital for socially and environmentally responsible investments as well as facilitating accelerated market entry as a strategic partner to companies attempting to penetrate the “barriers to entry” in the Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC) countries, specifically Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, Oman, Qatar, Bahrain and the United Arab Emirates. IAA provides customized solutions to companies seeking assistance in identifying the most appropriate local partners in the GCC countries.

IAA has a formidable record of placing hundreds of millions of dollars in private equity, hedge fund, insurance and real estate investments in this region. Our innovative, cost-effective solutions provide clients with a competitive edge and enhance their operational effectiveness, trans-cultural congruence and experience. Our local market knowledge, navigational expertise and strategic partnerships are winning solutions for scaling businesses into the GCC countries, minimizing years of potential “downtime” and costly errors. We have excellent distribution capabilities throughout the Gulf Cooperation Council Countries, having successfully represented General Partners (GPs) in raising several billion USD since 1996. We offer ancillary services to GPs including investor relations, vetting potential strategic partners and investors through the final diligence and closing process.
